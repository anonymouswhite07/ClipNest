Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ClipNest. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our service.

2. Zero-Logs Policy

ClipNest operates on a strict zero-logs policy for media processing. We do not track, store, or monitor the URLs you submit, nor do we keep copies of the media you download. All processing is done temporarily in memory and is immediately discarded.

3. Data Collection via Third Parties

While we do not collect personal data directly, we use industry-standard third-party services to ensure the platform remains stable, secure, and free:

  • Google Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (like page views and platform popularity) to improve our service.
  • Google AdSense: We display ads to fund our servers. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

4. Cookies

We do not use proprietary cookies. However, third-party services (like Google AdSense and Analytics) use cookies to function. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

5. Intellectual Property

ClipNest is a tool designed to help users download media for fair use, personal archiving, or when they have explicit permission from the creator. We do not host any copyrighted content and are not responsible for the media users choose to download.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@clipnest.white0.me.
